About Me

Hi there! I'm Willow Laing, a passionate Web Developer both professionally and in my free time. Building fun, funky and easy to use websites is my specialty, using my fluency in HTML and CSS (as well as PHP and JavaScript sprinkled in!). I love to work side by side with clients to make their vision come to life, as well as assuring a smooth experience for their users. I can't wait to hear from you!

More About Me

Currently, I'm a student at Fanshawe College, specifically their Web Development and Internet Applications program. I've enjoyed working through practical projects in many modern languages and frameworks, including WordPress, JavaScript, PHP, React, Angular and more. I'm based in Sarnia, Ontario, where I've lived with my cat, dog and five of my siblings for the past few years.

My interests (besides web development) include writing, reading, and too much coffee. Most of my time even for fun is devoted to coding, where I've found I can indulge my creative side without trying to draw more than a stick figure! I also spend a fair bit of time traveling. Exploring different parts of the world is my (second) greatest passion.
